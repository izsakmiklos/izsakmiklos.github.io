About me

I am an Economist specialized in Content and Marketing based in Budapest. The design approach to the work I create is based on current trends and inspired by minimalism and geometry with focus on advertising efficiency. I adore the creative process; it is addictive and can give never ending possibilities. Marketing has always been a favourite, whether thats data analytics, social content creation or online strategy, I thoroughly enjoy doing so. I have a huge love for fashion and modern design which I like to combine within my work. Working with a team is a real treat for me, I love navigating between different ideas and create a mutual success. When not working with marketing, I enjoy getting to create and spend time on personal projects, where I recreate memories for people using lineart and their personal photos.
